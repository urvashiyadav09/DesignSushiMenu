Traditional Japanese Sushi Restaurant

Sushi is Japan's national dish consisting of a mixture of rice and vinegar, along with various toppings (such as fish or shellfish). It is a common misapprehension that all sushi contains or consists of raw fish. Raw fish is a key ingredient but are is not used in all types of sushi. Sushi can also, for example be deep-fried (tempura).
